Majira Watches is a Kenyan watch brand

 

At Majira Watches, we craft timepieces that blend modern elegance with a spirit of playfulness; think dhow dancing on the indian ocean waves.

Under the beauty lies care, precision, durability and high-performance watches that both seasoned collectors and curious newcomers can appreciate. And just like the Swahili proverb says, “Chema chajiuza, kibaya chajitembeza”, we let the quality speak for itself. We profoundly value beauty that outlives trends. To us, our watches aren't just another item, it’s something that holds meaning, renews its relevance over tiime and continues to earn its place in daily life, year after year.

Our inspiration comes from the rich cultural mosaic of East Africa where Swahili proverbs meet khanga patterns, carved doors, and highland textiles tell stories across generations. From the coastal chill vibes to the urban rhythm of our cities, our savannahs, we blend these traditions into timepieces that are modern yet rooted, playful yet profound. Each watch is a tribute to the beauty, craft and spirit of our region.

Our journey has just began with a dream and the spirit of “pole pole ndio mwendo.” At MTI, we will continue to incorporate East African inspired designs in our collections and ensure that every watch you wear, has you feeling part of a movement that celebrates local identity.  

 

Asante kwa kutusindikiza kwa safari hii.
